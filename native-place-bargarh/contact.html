Discover the Charm of Bargarh

A landscape of fertile plains, mythological heritage, and artistic mastery.

The Pride of Western Odisha

Bargarh is globally recognized for **Dhanu Jatra**, the world's largest open-air theater. For 11 days, the entire town transforms into the mythological city of Mathura.

As the "Rice Bowl of Odisha," Bargarh's economy is powered by its vast, fertile agricultural lands, supported by the extensive canal system of the Hirakud project.

The district is also a spiritual beacon, housing the 14th-century **Nrusinghanath Temple** at the foothills of the medicinal Gandhamardan mountain range.

Culture

Famed for the Dhanu Jatra, Sambalpuri folk arts, and the Nuakhai harvest festival celebrated with immense devotion.

Tourism

Key sites include Gandhamardan Hills, Nrusinghanath Temple, Debrigarh Wildlife Sanctuary, and the scenic Papanga Hills.

Handloom

The heartland of the "Sambalpuri" textile, specifically the master weavers of Barpali and Bheden.
